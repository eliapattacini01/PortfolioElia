Satellite Predictive Diagnostics

Predictive diagnostics on large-scale telemetry time-series. This page focuses on engineering design, constraints, and measurable outcomes.

Goal

Detect early signals of abnormal behavior in spacecraft subsystems and increase the warning lead-time compared to traditional detection workflows, enabling earlier investigation and mitigation.

Key Constraints

Multi-satellite variability, non-stationary time-series, missing/irregular samples, class imbalance, strict reliability requirements, and the need to avoid data leakage with time-aware validation.

Impact

Built production-oriented ML pipelines for telemetry analytics, improving anomaly anticipation lead-time by weeks-to-months (depending on scenario) and producing deployment-ready outputs for stakeholders.

What I Built

End-to-End Pipeline

  • Telemetry ingestion and quality checks
  • Windowing and time-based feature extraction
  • Train/validation strategy to prevent leakage
  • Model evaluation and reporting outputs
  • Export formats for downstream use (tables/reports)

Data Engineering

  • Large-scale time-series processing
  • Reusable feature pipelines
  • SQL queries + MATLAB/Python workflows
  • Versioned datasets & reproducibility

Reliability Mindset

  • Handling of missing data
  • Robust evaluation and sanity checks
  • Clear reporting for non-ML stakeholders
  • Collaboration across teams/companies
