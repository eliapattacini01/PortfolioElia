Publication

Custom attention in transformer architectures for multiclass ECG beat classification.
Below is a short summary, key contributions, and how to cite the work.

Open DOI

What It’s About

I focused on a transformer-based models for ECG beat classification, focusing on improved attention mechanisms for time-series signals. The model targets multiclass classification with robust evaluation.

Highlights

  • Custom attention mechanism tailored to ECG morphology
  • Strong performance on multiclass beat classification
  • Emphasis on generalization and evaluation methodology

Results

Reported performance includes F1-score up to 0.93 and precision up to 0.95 on evaluated datasets, with a focus on stable performance across classes.

How to Cite

Citation

Pattacini E. et al., “Custom Attention in Transformer Architectures for Multiclass ECG Beat Classification”, Springer, 2025. DOI: 10.1007/978-3-031-96538-8_9
